DECORUS

What is decorus about ?

Decorus is an online review platform where critics give their reviews about their favourite movies which will help the watchers to decide if they want to watch a movie based on a genre they like. The decorus algorithm decides which review is the best based on the number of votes each review gets which helps the watchers get the most desired review.
